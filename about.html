About CGAPH

Nepal's premier digital gaming platform, revolutionizing the way gamers access top-ups, gift cards, and digital entertainment solutions. We're committed to providing seamless, secure, and instant digital gaming experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CGAPH emerged from a simple vision: to make digital gaming accessible to every Nepali gamer. What started as a small startup has grown into the country's most trusted digital gaming platform.

We recognized the challenges faced by Nepali gamers - limited access to international payment methods, high transaction fees, and delayed delivery times. CGAPH was built to solve these problems.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate gamers, tech enthusiasts, and customer service experts who understand the gaming community's needs. We're united by our love for gaming and commitment to excellence.

From developers to support staff, every team member contributes to creating the best possible experience for our customers.

Security & Trust

Security is at the core of everything we do. We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information.

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with international standards

Global Reach

While we're based in Nepal, our services reach gamers worldwide. We partner with leading international gaming companies to provide authentic, region-appropriate digital products.

Our platform supports multiple currencies and payment methods, making it accessible to gamers from different countries and regions.
