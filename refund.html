Refund Policy

Understanding our refund and cancellation policies

General Refund Policy

At CGAPH, we strive to provide the best possible service to our customers. However, due to the digital nature of our products, certain restrictions apply to refunds and cancellations.

Important Notice:

Digital products are generally non-refundable once delivered. This policy protects both our customers and our business from potential abuse.

Eligible for Refunds

The following circumstances may qualify for a refund:

  • Technical Issues: Product not working due to our system errors
  • Delivery Problems: Product not received within 24 hours
  • Wrong Product: Incorrect product delivered due to our error
  • Payment Errors: Duplicate charges or payment processing issues

Not Eligible for Refunds

The following circumstances do not qualify for refunds:

  • Product already used or redeemed
  • Customer changed their mind after purchase
  • Product working but not meeting expectations
  • Account banned or suspended by game developers
  • Regional restrictions or compatibility issues
  • Delays due to third-party services

Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Support: Email us at support@cgaph.com within 24 hours
  2. Provide Details: Include order number and reason for refund
  3. Evidence: Provide screenshots or proof of the issue
  4. Review Process: Our team will review within 48 hours
  5. Refund Decision: You'll receive notification of the decision

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 3-7 business days

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Service fees may be deducted
  • Processing fees are non-refundable
  • Partial delivery issues may qualify for partial refunds

Chargebacks and Disputes

Important:

Filing chargebacks without contacting us first may result in account suspension and legal action. Always contact our support team first to resolve any issues.

Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional situations may arise. Our customer service team is authorized to make case-by-case decisions for:

  • Long-term customers with good standing
  • Documented technical difficulties
  • Extenuating circumstances

Prevention Tips

How to Avoid Refund Issues

  • Verify game compatibility before purchase
  • Check regional restrictions
  • Ensure correct account information
  • Read product descriptions carefully
  • Contact support before making large purchases
